About

Root beer is a soft drink made from the root bark of the sassafras tree.
Root beer is typically, but not exclusively, non-alcoholic, caffeine-free, sweet, and carbonated. Like cola, it usually has a thick and foamy head. Unlike cola, it isn’t pure evil.
Root beer was commercially invented in the 18th century, but was made well before then as medicine for Native Americans.
Charles Elmer Hires was the first to successfully commercialize root beer in 1876. Later on, Pepsi started making root beer to rival against Hires’, labeled Barq’s.
